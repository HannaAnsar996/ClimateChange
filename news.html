Global Renewable Energy Capacity Surpasses Fossil Fuels for First Time

By Sarah Johnson May 8, 2025 5 min read

In a historic milestone for climate action, global renewable energy capacity has officially exceeded that of fossil fuels, according to the latest report from the International Energy Agency (IEA). Solar and wind power installations accelerated at an unprecedented rate in the first quarter of 2025, driven by falling costs and ambitious government policies worldwide.

"This represents a fundamental shift in the global energy landscape," said IEA Executive Director Fatih Birol. "The transition to clean energy is happening faster than many predicted, though we still need to accelerate efforts to meet climate goals."
